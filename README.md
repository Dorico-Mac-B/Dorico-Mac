## Overview

Dorico Professional Suite is a complete music notation and production platform that serves professional composers, arrangers, and publishers with the engraving depth, part management, and playback sophistication needed for commercial music notation at the highest quality level. It handles the full workflow from initial sketch through orchestration, part extraction, and final engraved output in a single application. The application integrates with Steinberg's audio engine for professional-quality playback and rendering.

Part layout management generates instrumental parts from the full score automatically, with independent formatting, page layout, and cue placement that produces performance-ready parts without maintaining separate files. Part-specific layout overrides handle the unique requirements of individual parts — page turns at rests, multi-bar rest consolidation, ossia staves — while remaining linked to the score for content synchronization. Changes to the score propagate to parts automatically, eliminating the synchronization burden that separate part files create.

Condensing automatically combines multiple instrumental staves onto shared staves when their music allows it, producing conductor's scores that follow professional publishing conventions without maintaining condensed and uncondensed versions separately. The condensing engine handles voice merging, divisi passages, and labeling according to configurable rules that match the conventions of major publishers. This automation handles one of the most labor-intensive aspects of orchestral score preparation.

Flow-based project structure organizes movements, songs, exercises, or any other musical units as independent flows within a single project, enabling multi-movement works, method books, and collections to exist in one file with independent numbering, instrumentation, and layout settings per flow. Flows can share or differ in instrumentation, and the layout engine handles flow distribution across frames and pages with automatic or manual control. This architecture is fundamentally more flexible than the single-document model of older notation applications.

Dorico Professional Suite is the notation platform for professionals who demand the highest engraving quality, the most intelligent automation, and the most flexible project structure available in a modern notation application. Its architecture, built from decades of notation software experience, addresses the real-world challenges of professional music publishing with solutions that legacy applications cannot match.

## Performance & Integration

Dorico is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while Dorico processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows
